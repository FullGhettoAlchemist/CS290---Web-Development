Pasta Aglio e Olio

The ultimate lazy dish. You should probably have all these ingredients in your kitchen at all times anyway.

    Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti or bucatini
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic - coined
  • 1 tablespoon or more of red pepper flakes
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped parsley
  • Juice from half a lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
    Special Kitchenware Needed:
  • Nada
    Instructions:
  1. Boil a big pot of water and cook your pasta.
  2. In the meantime, chop your garlic and parsley.
  3. When your pasta is about 3 mins from being done cooking, start heating up your olive oil in a large cast iron or nonstick pan.
  4. Once the pan is really hot, turn off the heat and drop your garlic in the oil. Keep them moving or you’ll burn them. Cook until golden brown.
  5. Add the red pepper flakes to the party.
  6. Using tongs, transfer your pasta into the pan. This will add enough pasta water usually, but add more if you feel that it is need.
  7. Throw the parsley on top and squeeze that lemon.
  8. Mix everything around and season taste with salt and pepper.
  9. If you really want to get fancy, take a carving fork and twirl the pasta around it to plate.
  10. Bon appetit!
