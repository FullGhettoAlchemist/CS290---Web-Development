Agedashi Tofu

This dish is commonly served as an appetizer. Very simple, very delicious.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of dashi (can make this from scratch or with dashi powder)
  • Silk tofu
  • Ginger - grated
  • Scallions - thinly chopped
  • Katsuobushi
  • Potato starch
    Special Kitchenware Needed:
  • Deep fryer (a small deep pot will work too)
    Instructions:
  1. Make your dashi by your preferred method, either by using the powder, or by steeping kombu, katsuobushi and a little soy sauce.
  2. Cut your tofu into 1x1x2 inch piece. Dry your tofu with a paper towel.
  3. Coat your tofu with potato starch and deep fry it until golden brown.
  4. To assemble, put some dashi into a bowl, lay some of the tofu into it, and top with some grated ginger, scallions and a couple flakes of katsuobushi.
